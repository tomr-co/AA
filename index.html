Amsterdam Automotive

Amsterdam Automotive is a small, locally owned and operated mechanical workshop located in Melling, Lower Hutt offering a comprehensive range of services from Warrant of Fitness checks to full servicing and repairs. We specialise in both old and new European vehicles, however all makes and models are welcome. At Amsterdam Automotive we take pride in offering all our customers one-on-one service in an honest and friendly environment to ensure you get the most out of your vehicle.

Personal Service

As a sole charge mechanic, owner Geoffrey Hill gets to know all his customers and their vehicles, offering a personalised service with honest and open communication. No repair work is ever undertaken without prior consultation with the customer.

Since 87

Amsterdam Automotive was founded in the late 1980s by two Dutch friends who saw a gap in the market for a specialist European mechanical workshop in Lower Hutt. Geoff came to work for them in 2003, and when the opportunity arose to purchase the business in 2013 it was an easy decision to take on the dedicated customer base, goodwill and can-do attitude of the original owners.

We want all our customers to feel welcome and valued at Amsterdam Automotive. We provide a comfortable waiting area with a good selection of magazines, a spacies machine to pass the time and coffee on request.

European Specialists

As European specialists we have a network of suppliers throughout the country offering access to genuine and aftermarket parts at competitive prices to suit your needs. We have experience in the maintenance and repair of a wide range of old and new European vehicles.
